## What it is

A free tool for Fantasy Premier League head-to-head leagues. Enter a league ID to see
standings, results, head-to-head records and season stats — no account needed.

## How it works

Standings show the full league table: wins, draws, losses, points for, recent form and
live win/loss streaks, updated automatically during gameweeks. Results are browsable by
gameweek or month, with the monthly view highlighting the top scorer, biggest win margin
and manager of the month. Head-to-head records cover the complete history against each
opponent — wins, losses, net points differential, full match history and a season
verdict. Stats round it out with season-wide leaderboards: highest individual score,
biggest single win margin, longest winning streak and most points scored overall.
